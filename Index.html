1. The PBM Friction Matrix

Proactive tracking matrix designed to mitigate critical failure points before they trigger client escalations.

Eligibility & Onboarding Delay or mismatch in daily eligibility file feeds (834 files) from the employer's HRIS to the PBM. 3. Rapid Triage Blueprint

Operational action plan triggered when eligibility errors prevent active plan members from receiving care.

1

Triage & Contain

Within 1 Hour

Acknowledge client escalation. Coordinate with IT data operations to review the transmission logs of the most recent 834 eligibility file feed.

2

Root Cause Analysis

Within 4 Hours

Isolate the root cause: investigate if the failure stems from a file transmission breakdown, a formatting rejection, or client HRIS database mismatch.

3

Manual Safeguards

Same Day

Coordinate overrides directly with the pharmacy helpdesk. Ensure standing members receive their medications immediately while system repairs take place.

4

Resolution & RCA

Within 24-48 Hours

Reprocess corrected data runs. Deliver a transparent incident report detailing operational steps implemented to prevent subsequent occurrence.

Client Account: Acme Corp (5,000 covered lives)

The Challenge: A 14% spike in Q2 specialty spend was traced to three members starting high-cost oncology therapies. These claims bypassed our preferred specialty network, leading to missed manufacturer rebate triggers.
